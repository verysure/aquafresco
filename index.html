About AquaFresco

Reinventing laundry. The current cleaning process utilizes significant amount of water and detergent to remove less than 1% of hydrophobic “garbage” (grease or stain). This highly inefficient laundry process makes an average Marriot hotel spend $10,000 on water and detergent every single week. AquaFresco provides the state-of-the-art filtration technology which regenerates 95% of laundry wastewater. This next generation laundry device would save $0.5M/yr for hotels, capturing a market opportunity of $200M per year. We also envision applications in commercial laundry, household, military and industrial wastewater treatment, revolutionizing the current water using technology.
