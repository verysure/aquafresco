About AquaFresco

Reinventing laundry. AquaFresco is reinventing the $50B cleaning market with its breakthrough wastewater recycle system. The company spun-off from the winning team of the MIT MADMEC competition in 2014. Since then, AquaFresco has been pushing the water recycle technology from a lab innovation to a commercialized product. The AquaFresco technology covers two different type of system: A water and detergent saving system, and a water saving system. For the water and detergent saving system, we have shown that it can save over 60% detergent used for a commercial machine and 25% of water. For the water save system, we can save 80% of water for commercial operator and 60% water for residential laundry. AquaFresco has full ownership of the IP for the patent-pending innovations. The water saving technology is not limited to laundry water use, it can also be potentially applied to other cleaning sectors such as car-wash, and other industrial wastewater cleaning process. Should you interested in licensing and acquiring the technology, please contact the team using the form in the contact section.
